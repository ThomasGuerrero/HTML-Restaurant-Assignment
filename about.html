Origin Story

Our origin started in Dallas, Texas; where many software developers were quite hungry, but needed to code at the same time. We decided to put both of those purposes together within this restaurant. We at H-Thai-ML hope to bring everyone an enjoyable meal, aswell as provide programmers and software developers with a comfortable area to conduct their work.
